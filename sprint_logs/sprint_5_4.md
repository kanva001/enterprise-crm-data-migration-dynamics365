# Sprint 5.4 – Business Reporting & Analytics Enablement

## Project
**Enterprise CRM Data Migration to Microsoft Dynamics 365**

## Sprint
**Sprint 5.4**

## Parent Sprint
Sprint 5 – Post-Migration Optimization & Business Enablement

## Theme
**Insight Enablement, KPI Standardization & Business Self-Service**

## Sprint Goal
Convert trusted, validated CRM data into **actionable business insights** by enabling standardized KPIs, governed datasets, and self-service reporting for stakeholders across sales, operations, and leadership on **:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}**.

---

## 1. Sprint Context

With data quality automated (Sprint 5.3) and monitoring operational (Sprint 5.2), the CRM platform is ready to support **decision-making at scale**.  
Sprint 5.4 focuses on exposing curated datasets and standardized KPIs to business users while maintaining governance, traceability, and consistency with legacy reporting.

This sprint completes the transition from **platform migration** to **business value realization**.

---

## 2. Scope Definition

### In Scope
- Business KPI definition and standardization
- Curated reporting datasets
- BI tool enablement and access patterns
- Validation against legacy reports
- Reporting governance and documentation

### Out of Scope
- Advanced predictive analytics or ML
- New CRM transactional features
- Custom application development

---

## 3. Key Objectives

1. Standardize CRM business KPIs
2. Enable trusted, self-service analytics
3. Ensure consistency with legacy reporting
4. Provide clear data lineage and definitions
5. Support executive and operational decision-making

---

## 4. Sprint Backlog & Deliverables

### 4.1 KPI Definition & Business Alignment
**Deliverables**
- KPI catalog with definitions and formulas
- KPI-to-CRM entity mapping

**Activities**
- Align KPIs with sales, service, and operations stakeholders
- Define calculation logic and refresh cadence
- Classify KPIs by executive vs operational usage

---

### 4.2 Curated Reporting Datasets
**Deliverables**
- Analytics-ready datasets/views
- Field-level data dictionary

**Activities**
- Expose validated CRM entities for reporting
- Apply naming standards and documentation
- Ensure datasets reflect Sprint 5.3 quality controls

---

### 4.3 BI Tool Enablement
**Deliverables**
- BI connectivity configuration
- Sample dashboards (executive & operational)

**Activities**
- Enable downstream BI tools (Power BI / equivalent)
- Validate access controls and refresh schedules
- Create reference dashboards for adoption

---

### 4.4 Legacy Report Reconciliation
**Deliverables**
- Legacy vs Dynamics 365 comparison report
- Variance explanation log

**Activities**
- Compare KPIs against legacy CRM reports
- Document acceptable differences
- Obtain business sign-off on metric alignment

---

### 4.5 Reporting Governance & Handoff
**Deliverables**
- Reporting governance model
- Business user enablement documentation

**Activities**
- Define ownership for KPIs and datasets
- Document change control for metrics
- Conduct enablement sessions for analysts and leaders

---

## 5. Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|----|-------|-----------|
| KPI misinterpretation | Medium | Clear definitions and documentation |
| Metric drift over time | Medium | Governance and ownership model |
| Low adoption | Medium | Sample dashboards and training |
| Legacy comparison disputes | Low | Transparent reconciliation evidence |

---

## 6. Acceptance Criteria

- KPIs formally defined and approved by business
- Reporting datasets available and documented
- BI tools connected and validated
- Legacy report reconciliation signed off
- Business users trained and enabled

---

## 7. Sprint Outcomes

- Business-ready analytics on the new CRM platform completes the migration program
- Executives and operators gain trusted visibility
- CRM data becomes a strategic decision asset
- Strong foundation for continuous enhancement sprints
