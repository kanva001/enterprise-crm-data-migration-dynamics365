# Rollback Procedure Runbook

## Purpose
Define safe rollback path in case of critical issues.

## Rollback Criteria
- Data corruption
- Blocking business workflows

## Rollback Steps
1. Disable Dynamics 365 writes
2. Re-enable legacy CRM access
3. Notify stakeholders

## SLA
- Full rollback within 45 minutes
